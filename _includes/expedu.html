Experience & Education

Timeline

  • 2004-2006

    Rysensteen Gymnasium

    I went to Rysensteen Gymnasium (High School) in the center of Copenhagen. I still think back on these three years as some of the best time I have ever had. Loads of friends and stuff to do. My focus was on Math, Physics, English and Music (I play the drums). During this time I obtained a position at the Parker Institute, Frederiksberg Hospital, where I would be working for the next 10 years.

  • 2007-2011

    B.Sc. Physics

    I had a great time studying physics at University of Copenhagen. After finishing the mandatory electromagnetism-, special relativity- and quantum mechanics courses I focused on mathematical modeling, programming, and medical imaging. An abstract of my bachelor’s thesis on knee cartilage segmentation was presented at EULAR 2011, London, and I gave an oral presentation at IEEE ELMAR 2011, Zadar, Croatia, which won the “Best Student Paper” award.

  • 2011-2014

    M.Sc. Eng., Medicin & Technology

    For my Master's, I specialized in machine learning and medical image processing. Being part medicine and part computer science, this degree provides a fantastic multi-disciplinary foundation for working in this field. And as a graduate in engineering I come with an open mind, new ideas and high standards for myself and the company I work for.

  • 2014-present

    Scientific Project Officer

    I currently work for Image Analysis in London, England, as a Scientific Project Officer. My responsibilities include developing the core image processing algorithms for Image Analysis' cloud-based MRI scoring platform, supporting clinical clients and keeping our servers up and running. It's a fantastic job. We are changing the future of DCE-MRI's role in cancer and inflammatory diseases diagnosis.
